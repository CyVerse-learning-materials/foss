**Before FOSS Starts** ==========

Please endeavor to complete the [pre-FOSS
setup](../before/installation.html) before arriving at FOSS.

FOSS runs under a [Code of
Conduct](../getting_started/code_of_conduct.html). Please familiarize
yourself with it.

FOSS Online sessions are recorded and recordings are posted on
CyVerse\'s private YouTube channel for participants to review at any
time (only those with the link can view).
